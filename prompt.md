# ForgeAcoustics Landing Page Prompt

## Overview

Create a dark, futuristic, clean landing page for **ForgeAcoustics**, a visionary deep-tech startup building the next generation of ultrasound AI development infrastructure.

**Target audience:** Medical ultrasound researchers and investors  
**Primary goal:** Book a Demo

---

## Brand Positioning

ForgeAcoustics combines **physics-informed synthetic data** with **real-world ultrasound data** to rapidly build task-specific ultrasound algorithms and software.

**Core message:**
> Physics simulation forged with real-world data for tailor-made ultrasound AI.

The company should feel:
- Visionary
- Technically credible
- Investor-ready
- Research-driven
- Clean and high-end
- Futuristic (SpaceX-inspired, but medical)

---

## Visual Style

- Dark theme (black / graphite)
- Electric blue, cyan, or soft violet accents
- Clean geometric typography
- High contrast and lots of negative space
- Subtle ultrasound wave / simulation visuals

---

## Animation Direction

- Smooth scroll-based animations
- Elements fade and slide into view
- Subtle parallax effects
- Animated pipelines and waveforms
- Soft glow hover effects

Animations should feel premium and restrained.
