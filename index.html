Ultrasound expertise does not scale.

Scarce human expertise is the bottleneck

High-quality ultrasound depends on skilled operators, validated workflows, and subtle acquisition decisions that are difficult to reproduce at scale.

Delayed access means delayed diagnosis

Many diseases are detectable with ultrasound, but exams remain constrained by staffing, training, workflow capacity, and local availability.

The data problem still blocks automation

Clinical data is realistic but rarely has ground truth. Synthetic data has labels but must be fused with real-world scans before algorithms can survive deployment.

From scarce manual expertise to repeatable robotic acquisition.

ForgeAcoustics turns ultrasound AI development into a repeatable engineering process: simulation creates controllable worlds, real data anchors performance, and the resulting algorithms power software and robotic workflows.
