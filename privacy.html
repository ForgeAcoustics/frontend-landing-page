Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 27, 2026. ForgeAcoustics LTD is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal data when you visit our website, request a demo, or otherwise interact with us through the site.

Data Controller

Unless otherwise stated, ForgeAcoustics LTD is the controller responsible for processing personal data collected through this website. For privacy-related questions, contact info@forgeacoustics.com.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as contact details when you reach out or request a demo. We may also collect certain technical and usage data automatically when you use the site.

  • •Identity and contact data, such as your name, company, email address, phone number, and any information you include in messages
  • •Business and inquiry data, such as demo request details, product interests, or partnership information
  • •Technical data, such as IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, referring URLs, and approximate location derived from IP
  • •Usage data, such as pages visited, timestamps, clicks, navigation paths, and interactions with forms or booking links

How We Use Your Information

We use personal data to operate the website, respond to requests, schedule and deliver demos, communicate with prospective customers and partners, maintain site security, and improve our products and services.

Depending on the context, our legal bases may include your consent, the performance of steps requested before entering a contract, our legitimate interests in operating and improving the site and our business, and compliance with legal obligations.

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies, local storage, pixels, or similar technologies to enable core site functionality, understand traffic and usage patterns, and improve performance. Some tools may be provided by third parties such as analytics, hosting, scheduling, or embedded-content providers.

Where required by law, we will request consent before setting non-essential cookies. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings, although some site features may not function properly if disabled.

Sharing of Personal Data

We may share personal data with service providers who help us operate the site and our business, such as hosting providers, analytics providers, email tools, scheduling platforms, customer relationship management tools, professional advisers, and authorities where required by law. We do not sell personal data for money.

International Transfers

Your personal data may be processed in countries other than your own. Where we transfer personal data internationally, we will use appropriate safeguards required by applicable law, such as contractual protections or transfers to jurisdictions recognized as providing adequate protection.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including to respond to inquiries, maintain business records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations. Retention periods depend on the nature of the data and the context in which it was collected.

Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection to certain processing, and the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise any privacy rights, contact us at info@forgeacoustics.com.

Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. No method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for business and professional audiences and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children where prohibited by law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date on this page. Material changes will take effect when posted unless otherwise stated.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise a privacy right, contact us at info@forgeacoustics.com.
